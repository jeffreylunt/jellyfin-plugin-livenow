Live Now

Floats currently-watched ("warm") Live TV channels to the top of every user's guide (favorites) and badges them with a live viewer count (e.g. 🔥x3 ESPN HD), reverting the moment a channel goes cold. Everything runs in-process inside Jellyfin — no external service, instant float.

Master switch for the background loop (name badges + favorite float).
Sets IsFavorite on warm channels for every user so they rise to the top of the native guide. The plugin only ever removes favorites it added — a user's own favorites are never touched. Turn off to keep only the name badge.
How often to re-check active playback. Default 45.
How often to re-evaluate ALL warm channels (vs the cheap delta) to catch new users or a transient miss. Default 10 (~7.5 min at a 45s poll).
How often the Live Now web page auto-refreshes. Default 15.
